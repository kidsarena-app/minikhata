Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your financial data on servers.

Does MiniKhata work offline?

Yes. You can use all core features without internet.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions are handled securely through Google Play and can be canceled anytime.

Will I lose data if I uninstall?

Yes, unless you have taken a backup. We recommend exporting or backing up your data regularly.
