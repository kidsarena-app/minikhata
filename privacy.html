Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

MiniKhata ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the MiniKhata mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

MiniKhata does not collect, transmit, or store your financial ledger data on external servers. All transaction and ledger information is stored locally on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

All entries, transactions, and generated reports remain on your device. If you uninstall the app, locally stored data may be removed unless backed up by you.

3. Advertising

MiniKhata uses Google AdMob to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • App usage data

This data is collected by Google to provide relevant ads. For more details, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Subscriptions & Payments

Premium features are offered through subscription plans processed securely by Google Play Billing.

We do not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled through your Google Play account before the renewal date.

5. Exported Files

PDF reports generated by MiniKhata are saved to your device’s Downloads folder using Android system storage mechanisms. We do not access or upload these files.

6. Permissions

The app may request access to device storage solely for the purpose of saving exported PDF reports.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards within the app; however, no digital system is completely secure. Users are responsible for safeguarding their own device.

8. Children’s Privacy

MiniKhata is not directed to children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
